Welcome to Hanzi Driller

Available in both Simplified and Traditional Characters!

What is it?

Hanzi Driller is an application designed to train a Pavlovian response such that when you see a Chinese character, you instantly know how it's pronounced. To achieve this, you will be shown characters, and be prompted to type in the character's pronunciation and/or meaning. You can choose whether you want to drill only meanings, only pronunciations, or both. The goal is for you to go as quickly as possibly, and do multiple repetitions with each character until your response comes naturally, without thinking. Once you've selected your list, the application does all the work for you. All you have to do is type your responses, and the details are handled in the background.

How does it work?

You start by creating a list of characters to study with. Characters are numbered 1 to 6000, representing the 6,000 most common Chinese characters, in order of frequency. For example, if you want to drill the 500 most common characters, define your list as 1 to 500. The characters will be presented to you with the easiest (i.e., most common) characters first, and increase in difficulty as you master more characters. More advanced students should define their list with a higher starting point (e.g., 2000 to 3000). Your study list can be as large or as small as you want.

There is a built-in algorithm which will make sure that you will continue to learn new characters at a good rate, without letting you forget characters you have already learned or wasting time on characters you already know. All settings can be adjusted and fine-tuned for those who would like to customize their study experience.

Who is it for?

While Hanzi Driller is appropriate for any student of Mandarin Chinese, it is most suitable for intermediate and advanced learners. As an intermediate or advanced student of Chinese, you have often gained plenty of experience reading and mastered many Chinese characters, but you may find you often forget the pronunciation of certain characters which are less common. This application is designed to quickly identify the characters you struggle with and drill them until they are hard-wired in your memory. It is also a great tool for advanced students of Chinese who have a firm grasp of all the general use hanzi, but would like to expand their knowledge to less commonly used characters.

Hanzi Driller is intended to be a supplement for your Chinese study regimen -- not a replacement for it. While this application will help you cement difficult and elusive Chinese character pronunciations in your memory, it is no replacement for a well-balanced study program based on reading and listening to comprehensible input.
